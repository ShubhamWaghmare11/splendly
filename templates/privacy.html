Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect the following information:

  • Account information — your name and email address, provided during registration.
  • Expense data — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions you enter.
  • Usage data — basic information about how you interact with the app, such as pages visited and actions taken.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data we collect solely to operate and improve Spendly:

  • To provide and display your expense history and summaries.
  • To authenticate your account and keep it secure.
  • To diagnose bugs and improve the reliability of the service.

We do not use your data for advertising, and we do not sell it to any third party.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored on secure servers. We retain your account and expense data for as long as your account is active. If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our systems within 30 days.

4. Third-Party Services

Spendly may use third-party services to support its operation, such as hosting providers. These providers are contractually required to protect your data and may not use it for their own purposes. We do not integrate with any third-party analytics or advertising platforms.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out through the contact information provided on the site. We will respond within a reasonable timeframe.
